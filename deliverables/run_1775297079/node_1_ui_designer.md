Imagine a vibrant, engaging webpage designed to captivate young learners. The entire background is a deep, soothing dark gradient, subtly shifting from a cosmic indigo at the top to a rich, velvety violet at the bottom, creating a sense of depth and modernity.

At the very heart of the page, a large, prominent display card sits front and center. This central area is where the current letter shines. It's a beautifully rounded, glassmorphic card, slightly translucent so you can just make out the dark gradient background behind it. A soft, inner glow of a bright neon pastel color, perhaps a radiant aqua or a cheerful fuchsia, emanates from its edges, giving it a playful, almost magical quality. A subtle shadow beneath it lifts it off the page, adding to the ultra-modern aesthetic. The letter itself, perhaps a giant, friendly 'A', is rendered in a bold, playful yet perfectly readable child-friendly font, filling much of the card's space.

Below this central display, stretching across the lower portion of the screen, is a meticulously organized grid of exactly twenty-six clickable buttons. Each button represents a letter from A to Z. Like the central display, every one of these letter buttons is a rounded, glassmorphic card. Each features its own unique bright neon pastel hue—think vibrant lime green, sunny yellow, electric orange, and sky blue—creating a rainbow effect across the grid. They too have that subtle translucency and delicate shadow, making them feel like polished, interactive gems. The letters on these buttons are in the same delightful, readable font as the central display.

Clearly positioned in the bottom right corner of the screen, a 'Next Letter' button stands out. This button also adopts the rounded, glassmorphic card style, perhaps glowing with a captivating electric purple or a joyful coral neon pastel color. Its text is clear and inviting in the playful, readable font.

When a child clicks on any of the letter buttons in the grid, a delightful and quick animation occurs. The chosen letter button doesn't just change state; it gives a satisfying, energetic little 'bounce' and simultaneously emits a bright, quick 'glow' that pulses outwards briefly before settling back into its normal, ready-to-be-clicked appearance. This visual feedback makes every interaction feel rewarding and fun.
