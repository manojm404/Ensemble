Imagine a vibrant, futuristic learning landscape. The webpage background is a deep, soothing dark gradient, transitioning smoothly from a rich indigo at the top to a twilight violet at the bottom, providing a calm contrast for the lively elements.

At the very center of the screen, a large, inviting display area dominates. This is a rounded, glassmorphic card, softly translucent, allowing a hint of the dark gradient background to show through, and casting a subtle shadow that lifts it off the page. Within this card, the current alphabet letter appears in an expansive, playful yet perfectly readable child-friendly font, glowing with a bright neon pastel color like electric coral or vibrant aqua, making it the undeniable focal point.

Below this central letter display, neatly organized across the lower half of the screen, is a grid of exactly twenty-six individual letter buttons, one for each letter from A to Z. Each button is also a rounded, glassmorphic card, distinctively colored with its own bright neon pastel hue—think lime green, sunshine yellow, or bubblegum pink—making the entire grid a rainbow of interactive elements. These buttons share the same subtle translucency and soft shadow effect as the central display, creating a cohesive, modern look. The letter on each button is rendered in the same playful, child-friendly, highly readable font, ensuring clarity for young learners.

Tucked clearly into the bottom right corner of the screen, easily accessible, is a 'Next Letter' button. This button mirrors the glassmorphic style, with soft rounded edges and a gentle translucency, perhaps colored in a slightly different, equally bright neon pastel like a cheerful lavender or a brilliant sky blue, to stand out while remaining harmonious with the overall design.

When a child clicks on any of the letter buttons in the grid, that specific button doesn't just change color; it springs to life with a quick, delightful bounce animation, as if gently inflated for a moment before settling back into place, accompanied by a soft, ethereal glow that emanates briefly from its edges. This provides immediate, fun feedback, encouraging interaction and engagement. The fonts used throughout are designed to be friendly and inviting, with slightly rounded edges and a clear, open structure, ensuring every letter is easily distinguishable for early readers without appearing overly childish.
